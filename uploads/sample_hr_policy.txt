EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK AND HR POLICY
===============================

1. EMPLOYEE LEAVE POLICY

The organization provides employees with different categories of leave to ensure work-life balance and employee well-being.

Annual Leave Entitlement:

• 12 Paid Sick Leaves per calendar year
• 18 Earned Leaves per calendar year
• 10 Casual Leaves per calendar year

Leave Guidelines:

• Sick leave may be used in case of illness or medical emergencies.
• Medical certificates may be required for absences exceeding three consecutive days.
• Earned leave must be approved by the reporting manager at least seven days in advance.
• Casual leave can be availed for personal reasons and short-term requirements.
• Unused earned leaves may be carried forward to the next year subject to company policy.
• Employees on probation are eligible for leave on a prorated basis.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How many sick leaves are allowed?
A: Employees are entitled to 12 paid sick leaves per year.

Q: How many earned leaves are available?
A: Employees receive 18 earned leaves per year.

Q: Can unused earned leaves be carried forward?
A: Yes, unused earned leaves may be carried forward according to company policy.

2. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
========================

The organization supports flexible working arrangements where business requirements permit.

Guidelines:

• Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
• Work from home requests must be submitted through the employee portal.
• Employees must remain available during official working hours.
• Attendance and productivity standards remain applicable during remote work.
• Employees are responsible for maintaining internet connectivity and a suitable working environment.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How many days can I work from home?
A: Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Q: Do I need approval for work from home?
A: Yes, manager approval is required.

4. NOTICE PERIOD POLICY
=======================

Notice Period Requirements:

• Full-time employees are required to serve a 60-day notice period.
• Employees may request an early release subject to management approval.
• Buyout of notice period may be permitted based on company discretion.
• Employees must complete knowledge transfer activities before their final working day.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: What is the notice period?
A: The standard notice period is 60 days.

Q: Can the notice period be reduced?
A: It may be reduced with management approval.

==============================

Eligible Expenses:
